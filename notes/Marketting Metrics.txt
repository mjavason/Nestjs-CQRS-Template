CUSTOMER ACQUISITION METRICS
Lifetime Conversion Rate: The percentage of users who take a desired action (e.g., making a purchase).


REVENUE METRICS
Average Order Value (AOV): The average amount spent per order.

Revenue Per Visitor (RPV): The revenue generated per app/site visitor.

Cart Abandonment Rate: The percentage of users who add items to the cart but don’t complete the purchase.
 

RETENTION METRICS
Retention Rate: The percentage of customers who return to your app after their first purchase.

Churn Rate: The percentage of customers lost in a specific period.
